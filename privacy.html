Privacy

Privacy Policy

We collect only the data required to run accounts, discussions, moderation, and safety features.

Data We Store

  • Account information (email, profile settings, auth identifiers).
  • Community content (threads, replies, reports, messages).
  • Security and audit data for abuse prevention.

How Data Is Used

Data is used to provide forum functionality, moderation, personalization, and account protection. We do not sell personal data.

Retention

Posts and replies remain available while your account is active. Moderation and audit records may be retained longer for safety and abuse prevention.

Third-Party Services

Authentication and data storage are provided through Supabase infrastructure. Access to production data is restricted to authorized maintainers.

Data Requests

For correction or deletion requests, use the contact page and include your account email.
